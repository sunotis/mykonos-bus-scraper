Welcome to the
"Mykonos Bus Lines Map".
I created this resource to provide comprehensive information about public bus routes on the island of Mykonos.

Although I'm not a native of Mykonos and don't speak Greek. I recognized the need for a clear, concise, and user-friendly bus map. My goal is to offer a compact yet informative guide that's easy to understand.

The map currently shows major stops, but I aim to include all intermediate stops in future updates. I've compiled this information from my personal experiences on the island and the official bus company website.

I strive for accuracy, but if you notice any errors or have suggestions for improvement, please email me at mykonosbusmap@gmail.com. Your feedback is crucial in enhancing the map's precision and usefulness.

This map is freely available for public use without any attribution requirements.

Given the map's growing popularity, I'm open to displaying high-quality advertisements that align with the website's aesthetic. These ads will be clearly marked as "Paid Advertisement" and seamlessly integrated into the site's design. If you're interested in advertising, please contact me for more information.
